This dataset contains information on used cars, including various attributes that describe each car's specifications, condition, and pricing details. 

Dataset Structure
The dataset contains 5848 rows and 14 columns. Each row represents a unique car entry with details on the make, model, specifications, and sale price.

Columns
Name: Name of the car, including the make, model, and variant.
Location: City where the car is available.
Year: Year of manufacture.
Kilometers_Driven: Distance the car has traveled in kilometers.
Fuel_Type: Type of fuel used by the car (e.g., Petrol, Diesel).
Transmission: Type of transmission (e.g., Manual, Automatic).
Owner_Type: Type of ownership (e.g., First, Second).
Mileage: Fuel efficiency of the car (in km/l or km/kg).
Engine: Engine capacity in cubic centimeters (CC).
Power: Engine power in brake horsepower (bhp).
Seats: Number of seats in the car.
New_Price: Original price of the car when it was new (if available).
Price: Current price of the car.
